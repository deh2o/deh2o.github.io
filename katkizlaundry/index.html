about Us

Cleanliness is nearly to Godliness.

At KatKiz Laundry, we understand that life can get busy. That's why we're here to simplify your daily routine and ensure quality delivery of our services to you.

  • Our Story

    KatKiz Laundry was born out of a passion for impeccable excellence and a dedication to making our customers' lives easier. With years of experience in the laundry and dry cleaning industry, we set out to create a service that combines the traditional values of quality and precision with modern convenience.

  • Our Commitment

    Katkiz Laundry is committed to delivering more than just normal services, We're dedicated to providing you with peace of mind, knowing that your favorite outfits, delicate linens, and cherished garments are in expert hands.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is simple: to be your trusted partner in enhancing your quality of life through impeccable delivery of quality, excellence, convenience, and sustainability.
